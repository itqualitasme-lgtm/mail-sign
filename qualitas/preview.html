With the white-card wrapper, the signature stays readable on a dark background. The card looks like a business card pinned to the email, independent of the recipient's theme. This approach works in ~95% of email clients (the main exception is Apple Mail iOS, which inverts everything aggressively — but inverted, the white card becomes a dark card with light text, so it's still readable, just inverted).
