Qualitas Signature — Dark Mode Variants

Four approaches to handling dark mode email clients, each with trade-offs. Compare the rendering, then tell me which one you want as the official version. All four use the same content (name, contact info, UAE banner) — only the visual treatment differs.

Variant A — White card (current v3)
Pure white background, grey border, red top stripe. Industry standard for email signatures. Always readable.
